Our Story

Where Chennai
Gathers

Tucked on Habibullah Road in the heart of T. Nagar, Stop 5 is more than a mess — it's a ritual. Locals drop in for a tumbler of filter coffee before work, students linger over chai, and families return for the warmth that only a neighborhood joint can offer.

Desi in all sense. No frills, no pretense. Just honest food, fast service, and a kitchen that opens until 11 PM — because good hunger doesn't keep hours.
